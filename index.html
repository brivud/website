About Us

Brivud Aluminium and Construction Works (U) LTD is a company which deals in everything that comes under Aluminium, Steel and Construction umbrella. We work with total commitment to help turn your dreams and ideas into solid images whether it's about beautifying your home with artistically-designed glass patterns or manufacturing aluminium windows and doors for commercial complex you are constructing.

Located in Naalya-Maikas House next to Mogas Fuel Station, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation in the construction industry.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to transforming your vision into reality, whether it's for residential, commercial, or industrial projects.

Vision

Refining construction with aluminum, steel, and glass decoration to create stunning structures.

Mission

To undertake all kinds of aluminum, steel, and construction projects with a commitment to delivering the best quality, ensuring utmost security, and incorporating beautiful designs. We achieve this by leveraging the latest technological advancements and state-of-the-art equipment.

Objective

To explore and apply advanced technologies in aluminum, steel, and construction works, driving modernization and monetization in the millennium era.
